LASAGNA

Description

Lasagna is a delicious dish prominently cooked in italy Its a popular dish among people and is also simple to cook!

Ingredients

  • tender sheets of pasta
  • ricotta cheese filling
  • rich meaty tomato sauce
  • Garlic and Onion
  • Olive oil

Steps to follow:-

  1. Preparation

    2. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).Boil a large pot of water. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  2. Prepare the Sauce

    3. In a skillet or saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add finely chopped onions and minced garlic. Sauté until onions are translucent. Add the ground beef or sausage (or vegetables/tofu for a vegetarian option) and cook until browned. Pour in the tomato sauce. Season with Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 10-15 minutes.
